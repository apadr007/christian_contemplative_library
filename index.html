The God Who Indwells

A Digital Exhibition of Christian Contemplative Pointers

This digital exhibition gathers thirty contemplative "shewings" from across the Christian tradition — pointers not to miraculous external events, but to the most radical claim of Christian mysticism: that the living God indwells the human soul, and that this indwelling can be consciously entered. Click any entry to open the full record, with its narrative, key quotes, Scripture reference, and a contemplative practice.
