The God Who Indwells

A Digital Exhibition of Christian Contemplative Pointers

This digital exhibition gathers 240 contemplative pointers from across twenty centuries and eleven streams of the Christian tradition — from the Desert Fathers to Eastern Orthodox hesychasm, from Celtic thin places to Ignatian discernment, from Protestant mysticism to the Liturgical tradition. Each entry points toward the same radical claim: that the living God indwells the human soul, and that this indwelling can be consciously entered. Click any entry to open the full record.
